How we work?

1

Application

Click on the Get a loan button and fill the loan application in a seamless
documentation process. The application includes necessary information,
such as revenue, registered business name, and the purpose of the loan.

Documentation and Verification

Upload required documents, such as business identification proof,
revenue statements, bank statements, and collateral details (if applicable).
This would go through a verification process with to confirm eligibility
for the loan.

2

3

Eligibility Requirement

A eligibility assessment will be conducted to evaluate your
creditworthiness and ability to repay the loan. This process involves
analysing your bank statement, valid means of identification, bank
verification number, CAC document, and other factors.

Loan Approval

After you meet the necessary lending criteria and passes the credit
assessment, the loan is approved. You will determines the loan amount,
interest rate, repayment term, and any associated fees.
